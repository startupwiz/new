About

We are educated in the technology field, cloud certified, an experienced in e-commerce solutions. The Startup Wiz understands the importance of a good technical foundation for businesses to flourish and we aim to consult with people on a personal level to bring their ideas to fruition.

After an initial consultation, work will begin immediately and communication will remain open to make sure your requirements are being met.

 

If you prefer to have a simple online store, your website can be done in 3 days.

Simple online stores only require a one time payment.
Cloud-based setups charge motnly and are low cost. You only pay for the storage, virtual hardware, and resources you         use. 

 

Basic online stores will remain available for a year.
Cloud-based businesses will stay functional for as long as you want.
